What We Offer

Our camp is designed for kids aged 8-12, where they will learn to code, build robots, and compete in exciting challenges.

Camp Details

Dates: Monday – Thursday, July 7 – August 13

Time: 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM (includes a 30-min lunch break at 12 PM and prayer time)

Price: $250 per 4-day week

Location: Pillars Preparatory Academy, 34 Charles St, South River, NJ 08882

Key Features

  • Hands-on learning with Spike Prime and Sumo Bots

  • Teamwork and problem-solving skills development
  • Medals and prizes for top teams
